Immersive Technologies

Immersive technologies refer to technologies that create a sense of immersion in a particular environment or activity. These technologies aim to simulate a realistic experience, often engaging multiple senses such as sight, hearing, and touch.

These cutting-edge tools use virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and mixed reality (MR) to make you feel like you're inside a video game, a historical era, or even a distant planet.
